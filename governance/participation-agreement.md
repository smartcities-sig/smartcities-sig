# Participation Agreement

## Purpose

This Participation Agreement defines the collaboration expectations for organizations and participants contributing to the Smart Cities SIG initiative.

The objective of the initiative is to support open, implementation-neutral, and reusable interoperability collaboration through publicly accessible discussions, Proof of Concept activities, and related ecosystem coordination efforts.

## Open Collaboration

The Smart Cities SIG operates as an open and collaborative ecosystem initiative.

Participants acknowledge that discussions, contributions, meeting records, and related outputs may be publicly accessible and may be reused as part of the initiative activities and related ecosystem collaboration efforts.

## Public and Non-Confidential Participation

Participants should not knowingly contribute:
- confidential information,
- proprietary restricted material,
- or content that cannot be publicly discussed or reused within the initiative.

Participants acknowledge that the initiative is intended to operate using transparent and publicly accessible collaboration practices.

## Contribution Rights and Intent

Each participant represents, to the best of their knowledge, that they have the appropriate rights or authority to contribute the material they submit to the initiative.

Participants acknowledge that contributions are intended to support open ecosystem collaboration and reusable interoperability activities.

## Reusable Outputs and Royalty-Free Collaboration Intent

The Smart Cities SIG is intended to support publicly reusable interoperability outputs and collaborative ecosystem alignment activities.

Participants should not knowingly contribute material that would restrict the open reuse of the initiative outputs or introduce royalty-bearing restrictions incompatible with the collaborative and reusable objectives of the initiative.

The initiative is intended to encourage practical and reusable interoperability collaboration across participating organizations and ecosystem stakeholders.

## No Obligation to Implement

Participation in the Smart Cities SIG does not create any obligation to:
- implement specific technologies,
- adopt specific interoperability approaches,
- publish specifications,
- or participate in future activities.

## No Transfer of Ownership

Unless otherwise separately agreed in writing, participants retain ownership of their individual contributions.

Participation in the initiative does not transfer ownership of intellectual property to the Open Mobile Alliance or other participants.

## Voluntary Participation

Participation in the Smart Cities SIG is voluntary.

Participants may join, contribute to, or discontinue participation in the initiative at their discretion, subject to applicable collaboration and governance practices.

## Relationship to Other Standards Activities

The Smart Cities SIG is intended to complement existing standards organizations, ecosystem alliances, interoperability programs, and industry initiatives rather than replace or supersede them.

Participants remain independently responsible for their own standards activities, organizational decisions, and related intellectual property considerations.

## Acceptance

By participating in the Smart Cities SIG, the participant acknowledges and agrees to the principles and collaboration expectations described in this Participation Agreement.
