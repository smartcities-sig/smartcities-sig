## 4. Working Model and Collaboration Approach
### Meeting Cadence
- One meeting per month:
  - May
  - June
  - July
  - August
  - September
  - October
- Proposed recurring schedule:
  - Wednesdays at 6:30 AM PT / 3:30 PM CEST

### Asynchronous Collaboration
Because the group will only meet once per month:
- Most technical work will happen between meetings
- Participants are expected to collaborate asynchronously during the month
- Meetings will focus on:
  - Review and alignment
  - Approval of proposals
  - Identification of next work items
  - Resolution of open issues

### Reference Working Process
- Review of:
  - [The Way We Work](/governance/the-way-we-work.md)
- Overview of:
  - Collaboration expectations
  - Open participation principles
  - Consensus-driven development
  - Contribution and review expectations
  - Cross-organization coordination model

### Decision-Making Process
- Transparency
- Impartiality
- Openness
- Consensus-driven collaboration

### Approval Process
- Topics should normally be presented in one meeting
- Review and approval targeted for the following meeting
