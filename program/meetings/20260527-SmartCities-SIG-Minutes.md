##### Process
- Decision-making process
  - Transparency --> Impartiality --> Openeness --> Consensus
  - Topics MUST be presented in one meeting, before being approved at the following meeting
- Release_Planning
  - [Timeline](https://github.com/smartcities-sig/smartcities-sig/blob/dev/program/roadmap/release-planning.md)
- Working methodology
  - Development work between meetings
  - Meetings:
    - Review & Approval
    - Next batch of development
- Contribution process
  - [CONTRIBUTING](https://github.com/smartcities-sig/smartcities-sig/blob/dev/CONTRIBUTING.md)
- PR and review workflow
  - Protected branches: `main` & `development (dev`)
  - Interim or Feature branches: members have write to create new branches but cannot merge on `main` and `dev`
- Meeting cadence
  - Meetings once a month: May, June, July, August, Sept, Oct
  - Wednesday's at 6:30 am PT, 3:30 pm CEST
